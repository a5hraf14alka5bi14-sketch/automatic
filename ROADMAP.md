# Roadmap — Automatic Restaurant OS

This roadmap tracks delivered milestones and upcoming priorities. Status legend:
✅ done · 🟡 in progress · ⬜ planned.

---

## Delivered

- ✅ JWT authentication with role-based access (admin / cashier)
- ✅ Dashboard with live stats
- ✅ Point of Sale (checkout, split bills, tax, loyalty redemption)
- ✅ Order management (dine-in / takeaway / delivery)
- ✅ Kitchen Display System with live WebSocket queue (mobile responsive)
- ✅ Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts and stock-movement audit
- ✅ Menu & recipes with recipe → inventory food-cost linking
- ✅ Customer management & loyalty points
- ✅ Reports & analytics (9 tabs incl. profitability, menu engineering, staff)
- ✅ Revenue forecasting & menu-engineering matrix
- ✅ AI Executive dashboard (OpenAI insights & daily summary)
- ✅ GitHub integration (repository sync)
- ✅ Notion integration (bidirectional project/task sync, Arabic status mapping)
- ✅ Official brand logo across app and printed outputs (PDF / receipts)
- ✅ Settings management (tax, tables, currency, loyalty rate)

---

## Next Milestones

- ⬜ Deployment automation & production health monitoring
- ⬜ ESLint + Prettier configuration and CI gate
- ⬜ Automated test coverage for API routes (currently business-logic only)

### Medium priority
- ⬜ QR code menu for table-side ordering
- ⬜ Multi-branch / multi-location support
- ⬜ Customer-facing online ordering portal
- ⬜ Real-time dashboard updates via WebSocket (beyond kitchen)

### Low priority
- ⬜ Mobile application (Expo)
- ⬜ Advanced AI: demand prediction, dynamic pricing
- ⬜ Supplier / purchase-order management
- ⬜ Internationalization beyond Arabic/English
